🔒 Privacy Policy

We take your privacy seriously. Here's everything we do (and don't do) with your data.

📅 Last updated: January 15, 2024

1. Introduction

PixelMotion AI ("we," "us," or "our") operates the PixelMotion AI website. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our service and the choices you have associated with that data.

2. Information We Collect

We collect minimal information to provide and improve our service:

  • Usage Data: We may collect information on how the service is accessed and used — this includes data such as pages visited, time spent, and browser type.
  • Image URLs: Image URLs you submit are processed by our AI service and are not stored on our servers after processing.
  • Generated Videos: Videos generated are temporarily hosted and automatically deleted after 48 hours.

We do NOT collect: your name, email, or any personal identifiable information unless you contact us directly.

3. Cookies & Tracking

We use minimal cookies for:

  • Storing your daily video usage count (stored locally on your device)
  • Analytics to improve our service (anonymized)
  • Advertising through Google AdSense (see section 5)

You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time.

4. How We Use Your Data

The data we collect is used to:

  • Provide and maintain our service
  • Monitor usage to detect and prevent abuse
  • Improve our AI video generation service
  • Display relevant advertisements

5. Advertising

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google AdSense may use cookies to personalize ads based on your browsing behavior. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings at adssettings.google.com.

6. Third-Party Services

We use the PiAPI service (powered by Google Veo 3.1) to process your image-to-video requests. Image URLs and prompts you submit are sent to this service. Please review PiAPI's privacy policy for information on how they handle your data.

7. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We do not store sensitive personal information.

8. Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes by posting the new policy on this page with an updated date.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us.
