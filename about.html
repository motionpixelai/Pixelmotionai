About Us

We Make AI Free for Everyone

PixelMotion AI was built with one simple mission — to give everyone access to powerful AI video generation, completely free of cost. No credit card. No account. No limits.

🎯 Our Mission

We believe that powerful AI tools should not be locked behind expensive subscriptions. PixelMotion AI is our commitment to making AI video generation accessible to every creator, student, business, and individual around the world — regardless of their budget.

🚀 How We Started

PixelMotion AI started as a passion project to solve a real problem — existing AI video tools were either too expensive or too complicated. We combined cutting-edge AI models with a simple, beautiful interface to create something anyone can use in seconds.

💡 How We Stay Free

We keep PixelMotion AI free by displaying non-intrusive advertisements and accepting optional donations from users who love the tool. Every coffee bought and every ad viewed helps us keep our servers running so you can create amazing videos for free.
