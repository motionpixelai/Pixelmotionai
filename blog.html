How to Write the Perfect AI Video Prompt

By PixelMotion AI Team

The most common mistake people make with AI video generation is writing vague, short prompts. The AI needs detailed instructions to create exactly what you have in mind.

The Perfect Prompt Formula

A great prompt has four parts: Subject + Motion + Camera + Style. Let's break each down:

  • Subject: What is in your image? (mountains, person, ocean)
  • Motion: What should move? (clouds drift, waves roll, leaves sway)
  • Camera: How does the camera move? (slow zoom in, pan left, steady)
  • Style: What is the mood? (cinematic, dreamy, dramatic, peaceful)

Good vs Bad Prompts

Bad: "Make it move"

Good: "Clouds drift slowly across the mountain peaks, gentle wind moves through the pine trees, slow cinematic zoom in, golden hour warm light, peaceful and majestic mood"

Pro Tips

  • Use descriptive adjectives (gentle, dramatic, soft, powerful)
  • Mention lighting conditions (golden hour, blue hour, overcast)
  • Describe the camera movement specifically
  • Include the overall mood or emotion
  • Aim for 50-150 characters for best results
